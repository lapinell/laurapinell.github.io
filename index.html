My Story

"Code is Poetry" - WordPress

It's the place where I can take all the pieces of me and combine it neatly into one working machine. My jill-of-all-trades skills becomes Developer-Designer-Teacher.

I started coding at a pretty young age - 7, maybe. I would watch my mom create websites using tables in DreamWeaver MX and wonder how she would change the colors and design this thing called a website so she could better sell her horses to clients across the country. So when she wasn't using her computer, I'd play on it - play with the code, explore it, break it, fix it, bend it, build it.

Ever since then I've played with code - that is until I realized I could turn this fun design puzzle into a career. Now it's puzzling together an even tiner jigsaw pieces: JavaScript and mySQL and PHP and CSS3 and HTML5; UX and UI and accessibility and so on and so forth.

I love the computer. The internet, really. I love art and math and science. I love helping people - individuals and groups.

I love code. Code is poetry.
